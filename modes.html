Summoner's Rift

The classic 5v5 mode that has multiple lanes and roles including a bottom lane, middle lane, top lane, and a jungle. The goal of the gamemode is to destoy the enemy teams nexus to do this players have to go down one of the 3 lanes completely destroying turrents and inhibitors in the way leading to the nexus.

Teamfight Tactics

TFT is an autobattler gamemode which is a turn based strategy game where you have to draft a team, position them, and fight against other teams until you are the last person standing.

ARAM

ARAM also known as ALL RANDOM ALL MID is a gamemode on league of legends that has an all random draft style where the champion that you receive is completely random and there is only one lane. it is another 5v5 format that also envolves destroying the enemy nexus.
