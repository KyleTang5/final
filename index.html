Whats league of Legends?

League of Legends is a MOBA game taht was created and developed by Riot Games in 2009 and it is a free to play game. The game features over 160+ different champions that can be played and it includes multiple gamemodes that you can play if you don't like on of them. League of Legends also has one of the largest E-Sports scenes for gaming.
